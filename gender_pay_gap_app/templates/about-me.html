Stephanie Sherman, PhD
   

I am a Data Science Fellow at Insight in New York City. I completed my PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience at the University of Texas at Austin and a postdoctoral fellowship at Boston College. Over the past 7 years I have enjoyed analyzing datasets to find meaning behind the numbers. My favorite part of conducting analyses is discovering new patterns and trends that lead to an objectively better understanding of a particular problem. I use python, R, bash scripting, and MATLAB to process and analyze a variety of human behavior, time series, surveys, and neuroimaging data.
